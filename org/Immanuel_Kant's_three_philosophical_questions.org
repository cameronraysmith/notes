#+TITLE: Immanuel Kant's three philosophical questions
* [[file:philosophy.org][philosophy]], [[file:Immanuel_Kant.org][Immanuel Kant]]
* Kant argues that all philosophy ultimately aims at answering these three questions:
** What can I know? 
** What should I do? 
** For what may I hope?
